ImmersiveOwl Antivirus

We have created a unique antivirus program to protect computers.

The ImmersiveOwl Antivirus engine is designed to detect malicious programs by analyzing different actions the file can take, as well as by sandboxing files and preventing them from harming the computer.

The ImmersiveOwl website is open source under the GPLv3 licence on GitHub. All work is provided free and open source with no warranty or liability.
